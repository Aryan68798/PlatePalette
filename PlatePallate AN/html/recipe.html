INSTRUCTIONS

01

PREPARE THE GLAZE

In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, ginger, and garlic. Ensure the sugar is fully dissolved. If you prefer a thicker glaze, add 1 tsp of cornstarch to the mix.

02

SEAR THE CHICKEN

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat with a splash of oil. Pat the chicken thighs dry and sear them skin-side down (if applicable) for 5–7 minutes until golden brown and crispy. Flip and cook for another 4 minutes.

03

DEGLAZE & SIMMER

Pour the prepared teriyaki mixture over the chicken. Bring to a simmer and let the sauce reduce and thicken until it coats the back of a spoon. Baste the chicken frequently with the bubbling sauce.

04

FINISH & SERVE

Slice the chicken into thick strips. Serve over a bed of fluffy jasmine rice. Drizzle with the remaining pan sauce and garnish generously with toasted sesame seeds and thinly sliced green onions.

🔥

PRO TIP

For the best flavor, marinate the chicken in half of the sauce for at least 30 minutes before cooking.

🍽

PAIR WITH

Steamed broccoli, bok choy, or a fresh smashed cucumber salad with chili oil.
